Our Mission

COMHECA is an independent, non-profit, humanitarian, non-political, non-governmental organization (NGO) in Bafut the North West region of Cameroon.
The registered office of COMHECA is located in Agyati Bafut.

Achieving the highest possible quality of life

Each individual will have personal goals that are important to their quality of life.

Maintaining self-care

To provide Home Health Care Services (HHCS) to the elderly in Bafut Sub Division North West Region of Cameroon.

Delaying decline

Older adults should be carefully monitored for any decline
